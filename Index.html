Top 5 Historical Places in India

Qutub Minar, Delhi

The Qutub Minar is a UNESCO World Heritage site in Delhi, India.
It is the tallest brick minaret in the world, standing at 73 meters tall, built by Qutb-ud-din Aibak in 1193.
